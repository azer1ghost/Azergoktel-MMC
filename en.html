ABOUT US

     AZERGOKTEL LLC which has operated in cable manufacturing industry since 2012, is a leading company in the sector, providing excellent customer service and prioritizing high quality products manufacturing. The industrial principle of Company is to manufacture wide range of cables, coating copper and aluminum wires with synthetic materials by means of modern equipments compliant with the requirements. The range of products include: Low Voltage Power Wires with PVC and Halogen free PVC insulation 450/750 V; Low Voltage Power Cables with PVC and Halogen free PVC insulation 300/500 V; Low Voltage Power Cables with PVC and Halogen free PVC insulation 0,6/1 kV; Low Voltage Power Cables with XLPE insulation 0,6/1 kV; High Voltage Power Cables with XLPE insulation 1 and 3 kV; Low Voltage Welding Cables with KQ insulation 0,6/1 kV; Low Voltage AER (SIP) Cables with Polyethylene insulation 0,6/1 kV; and High Voltage AES and Internal telephone cables without insulation. The company aims to honorably represent our country in domestic and foreign markets with its wide raneg of products meeting the requirements of our fast-growing republic. In this regard, manufacturing area has been advanced, and technological processing has been arranged by involving highly specialized personnel. The products, satisfying the requirements of global market, are manufactured under the principles of Good Laboratory Practices. The sales organization is complied with applicable regulations of import and export. In the Company, a special attention is paid to the environment and human health. Incorporated facilities meet the up-to-date standards, relevant measures are taken to prevent environmental pollution during manufacturing process, and personnel is provided with good working conditions. The mission of the Company is to bring the customer satisfaction to the highest level, meeting the demand on product and service by customer in domestic and foreign market, and to carry out the commitment focused on the improvement of people's living standard, prioritizing environmental and moral values as a cable manufacturer open for innovations and advancement. The Company has entered into various agreements with a number of intermediary companies from European countries and Russia, consequently it has been exporting applicable range of products to the same countries. Our Company makes it available to provide the selling of products in the domestic market along with meeting the product demand by foreign consumers.
